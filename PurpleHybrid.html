Purple Hybrid

A project begun in December 2014.


"Unable to accept his own demigodhood, a lonesome artist survives his own suicide attempt only to wake up in a persecuted aristocrat's mansion.

Never having known family, here he meets his cousin, who sought him out for years and rescued him from the brink of drowning with the help of the Count of the estate and his wife's powers.

Now amongst Demigods, five noble families who inherit powers from the spirit of their ancestor, his only cousin begs him to embark on a journey to find the remnants of their lineage. Ungrateful to still be breathing but curious of his kin, so begins his story to understand what it means to be greater than human."

Purple Hybrid is a Gothic fantasy renaissance visual novel.

The game currently boasts a script of over 125.000 words, more than 2 hours of soundtrack, and its illustrative artwork speaks for itself.
